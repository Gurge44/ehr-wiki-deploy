**Faction:** Crewmate\
**Alignment:** Vanilla\
**Basis:** Crewmate

The vanilla crewmate is not modified by Endless Host Roles. Every player will become a crewmate by default if no other roles are given a chance of being selected. Do your tasks, support the crew, and hope something exciting happens!

## Options

- ### Max
    **Type:** `Number`

    The cap for how many of this role can spawn.  
    <Aside>
    For the vanilla Crewmate role, this cap will be bypassed if not enough other roles are selected.
    </Aside>
