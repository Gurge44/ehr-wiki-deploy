## Role Description

The **Jester** is a Neutral Evil role. They win the game alone by successfully tricking other players into voting them out. The Jester may attempt to confuse or mislead others into ejecting them, often by acting suspiciously.

## Role Options

<details>
<summary>
### Max
</summary>
**Type:** `Number`\
**Default:** `1`

Sets the maximum number of Jesters that can appear in a match.
</details>

<details>
<summary>
### Can call emergency meetings
</summary>
**Type:** `Toggle`\
**Default:** `OFF`

Determines whether the Jester can call emergency meetings.
</details>

<details>
<summary>
### Can Vent
</summary>
**Type:** `Toggle`\
**Default:** `OFF`

Allows the Jester to use vents like Impostors.
</details>

<details>
<summary>
### Has Impostor Vision
</summary>
**Type:** `Toggle`\
**Default:** `OFF`

Grants the Jester the same vision range as Impostors.
</details>

<details>
<summary>
### Sunnyboy Chance
</summary>
**Type:** `Percentage`\
**Default:** `0%`

Specifies the chance for the **Sunnyboy** sub-role to spawn instead of the regular Jester.
</details>
