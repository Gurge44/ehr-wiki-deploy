## Role Description

The **Workaholic** is a Neutral Evil role. When the Workaholic completes its tasks, it will kill everyone other than itself, with the death reason **Ashamed**, and will win alone. The Workaholic may or may not be able to win after death, and may have its role revealed to everyone, depending on the host's settings.
