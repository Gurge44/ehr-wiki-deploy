## Role Description

The **Schrödinger's Cat** is a Neutral Benign role that has the ability to survive a kill attempt, subsequently becoming their attacker's role and winning alongside them. If configured to be fragile, they will take on the role of anyone who targets them with a kill ability.

**Related Roles:** [**Amnesiac**](../../benign/amnesiac)

## Role Options

<details>
<summary>
### Wins with Crewmates if Not Attacked
</summary>
**Type:** `Toggle`\
**Default:** `OFF`

Decides whether the Schrödinger's Cat wins with the Crewmates if they are not attacked during the game.
</details>

<details>
<summary>
### Steals the exact Impostor role instead of Renegade
</summary>
**Type:** `Toggle`\
**Default:** `OFF`

Decides whether the Schrödinger's Cat takes on the Impostor's exact role if attacked by an Impostor, instead of changing to the [**Renegade**](../../../impostor/madmate/renegade) role.
</details>
