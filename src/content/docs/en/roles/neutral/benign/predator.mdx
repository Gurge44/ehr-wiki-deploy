## Role Description

The **Predator** is a Neutral Benign role. **It stands out as one of the few Benign roles equipped with a killing ability.** The Predator is provided a list of roles, referred to as "prey," at the start of the game. They must successfully kill at least one of these roles to satisfy their win condition. If the Predator kills any player who is *not* one of their designated prey roles, the Predator will suicide.

## Role Options

<details>
<summary>
### Max
</summary>
**Type:** `Number`\
**Default:** `1`

Specifies the maximum amount of Predators that can appear in one game.
</details>

<details>
<summary>
### Amount of Roles to choose from
</summary>
**Type:** `Number`\
**Default:** `3`

Specifies the amount of roles that the Predator can choose from to kill one of them to satisfy their win condition.
</details>

<details>
<summary>
### Maximum Number of Impostor Roles to Choose From
</summary>
**Type:** `Number`\
**Default:** `1`

Specifies the maximum number of Impostor roles that can appear in the Predator's role choices.
</details>

<details>
<summary>
### Kill Cooldown
</summary>
**Type:** `Time`\
**Default:** `15s`

Specifies the Kill Cooldown of the Predator.
</details>

<details>
<summary>
### Can Vent
</summary>
**Type:** `Toggle`\
**Default:** `OFF`

Decides whether or not the Predator can Vent.
</details>

<details>
<summary>
### Has Impostor Vision
</summary>
**Type:** `Toggle`\
**Default:** `OFF`

Decides whether or not the Predator has Impostor Vision.
