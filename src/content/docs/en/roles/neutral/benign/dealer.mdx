## Role Description

The **Dealer** is a Neutral Benign role. The Dealer can use their kill button on a player to give them an add-on. The add-on given can be any add-on other than Betrayal add-ons and Role Specific add-ons. After the Dealer gives the specified amount of add-ons, their win condition is satisfied, and they win with the winning team, alive or not.

## Role Options

<details>
<summary>
### Max
</summary>
**Type:** `Number`\
**Default:** `1`

Specifies the maximum amount of Dealers that can appear in one game.
</details>

<details>
<summary>
### Add-ons Assign Target Sees The Add-on
</summary>
**Type:** `Select (On The Next Meeting, After 5 Seconds, Instantly)`\
**Default:** `On The Next Meeting`

Specifies when the target of the Dealer's ability is able to see the Add-on given.
</details>

<details>
<summary>
### Number Of Add-ons Needed To Give To Win
</summary>
**Type:** `Number`\
**Default:** `5`

Specifies the amount of add-ons the Dealer needs to give to satisfy their win condition.
</details>
