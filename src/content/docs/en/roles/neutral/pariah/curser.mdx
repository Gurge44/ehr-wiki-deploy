## Role Description

The **Curser** is a Neutral Pariah role. They can use their kill button on a player. Clicking it once reveals the target's **alignment** to the Curser, and clicking it twice applies a **random, permanent debuff** to the target.

**Related Roles:** [**Donut Delivery**](../../crewmate/support/donut-delivery)

## Role Options

<details>
<summary>
### Max
</summary>
**Type:** `Number`\
**Default:** `1`

Specifies the maximum amount of Cursers that can appear in one game.
</details>

<details>
<summary>
### Initial ability uses
</summary>
**Type:** `Number`\
**Default:** `1`

Specifies how many ability uses the Curser starts with.
</details>

<details>
<summary>
### Ability cooldown
</summary>
**Type:** `Time`\
**Default:** `15s`

Specifies how long the Curser has to wait to curse a player.
</details>

<details>
<summary>
### Impostor vision
</summary>
**Type:** `Toggle`\
**Default:** `OFF`

Decides whether or not the Curser has Impostor vision.
</details>

<details>
<summary>
### Can Vent
</summary>
**Type:** `Toggle`\
**Default:** `OFF`

Decides whether or not the Curser can Vent.
</details>

<details>
<summary>
### Permanent Lower vision
</summary>
**Type:** `Multiplier`\
**Default:** `0.3x`

Specifies the decreased vision multiplier applied when a player receives a curse.
</details>

<details>
<summary>
### Permanent lower speed
</summary>
**Type:** `Multiplier`\
**Default:** `0.7x`

Specifies the decreased speed multiplier applied when a player receives a curse.
</details>
