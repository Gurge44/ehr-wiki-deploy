## Role Description

The **Spirit** is a Neutral Pariah role with the ability to place a candle onto a player. If two players have candles, interactions targeting one player are swapped and applied to the other player instead.

**Related Roles:** [**Seamstress**](../../neutral/pariah/seamstress)

## Role Options

<details>
<summary>
### Max
</summary>
**Type:** `Number`\
**Default:** `1`

</details>

<details>
<summary>
### Shapeshift cooldown
</summary>

**Default:** `15s`
